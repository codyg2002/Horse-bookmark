Horse

Horses can sleep standing up or laying down.

  • Scientific Name: Equus caballus
  • Lifespan: 25-30 years
  • Weight: 120-2,200 lbs.
  • Habitat: Every continent except Antarctica

Horses have been domesticated for approximately 5,000 years. They are present worldwide, and fill many roles in areas such as work, sport, warfare, and even therapy. They provide raw material for many products including milk, meat,and leather. Their hair is used to make bows for stringed instruments. Few animals are as beautiful and majestic as the horse.
